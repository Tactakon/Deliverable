About Us

Welcome to Verge, the collaborative playlist web application created by a group of Georgia State University computer science students for our Capstone project.
Verge is a platform that allows you to share your music taste with your friends and discover new songs together.

At Verge, we believe that music is better together. That's why we created a collaborative community where you can work together in real-time with your friends to create the ultimate playlist. You can share your favorite songs and discover new ones that you may have never come across before.

Our web app is passcode protected, ensuring that only authorized collaborators have access to make changes to the playlist. This creates a private and secure playlist experience where you can feel confident in sharing your music preferences.

We designed Verge to make collaboration seamless and enjoyable, whether you're a professional playlist curator or just someone who enjoys good tunes. Our platform makes it easy for everyone to play a part in creating amazing playlists for any moment.

We are excited to invite you to join our Verge community and unlock the perfect soundtrack for any occasion. Music, made together.
