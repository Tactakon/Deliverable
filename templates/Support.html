Support

Verge is a collaborative playlist web application created by a group of Georgia State University computer science students for our Capstone project. Please note that this is a college project and not a real website, so we do not offer actual support services for this application.

If you have any questions or feedback regarding Verge, feel free to reach out to us via email at vergeproject.help@gmail.com. We will do our best to respond to your inquiries in a timely manner.

Thank you for your interest in Verge, and we hope you enjoy using our collaborative playlist application.
